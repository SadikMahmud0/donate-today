Our Donate Plan

We formed teams in 64 districts of Bangladesh I'm planning to help disaster victims in a variety of ways.

Food for a family

We will try to deliver food to every house in the disaster affected area through our district representatives.Your little help will put a smile on their face and feed them.

Shelter for a family

We will continue to work tirelessly to ensure the rehabilitation of the people affected by the disaster. We will do our best to help them physically and financially to ensure their rehabilitation. Their rehabilitation is guaranteed.

Education for a Child

We provide education to the children in the disaster affected areas. I try to study by helping them with money. May our next generation not be ruined. May they not grow up without humanity and learn to be by the side of people.
